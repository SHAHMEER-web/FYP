Bachelor of Science (BS) in Computer Science

Home BS Computer science MS Computer science Phd Computer science Faculty Computer science

Program Vision

To achieve excellence in quality education and research, preparing leaders in computational and allied sciences who contribute to industrial and societal development and prosperity.

Program Mission

  • Offer state-of-the-art undergraduate programs in Computer Science.
  • Provide an excellent working environment that motivates faculty and students.
  • Utilize expertise in relevant disciplines to help society solve real-life problems.
  • Develop entrepreneurship skills so students become problem solvers and innovative developers.

Program Educational Objectives (PEOs)

  • Establish a strong technical and professional foundation, enabling further studies in Computer Science.
  • Design software products required for daily life solutions.
  • Work professionally as team members or leaders in various professional environments.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Minimum 50% marks in Intermediate (HSSC) with Pre-Engineering, Pre-Medical, relevant DAE, or equivalent qualification.
  • Pre-Medical students must pass deficiency courses in Mathematics within the first two semesters.

Fee Structure

  • Admission & Enrollment Fee (One-Time): PKR 18,500
  • Registration & Exam Fee (Per Semester): PKR 16,700
  • Tuition Fee (Per Semester): PKR 74,800
  • Total Per Semester: PKR 110,000
