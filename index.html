Department of Computing

HOD's Message

Welcome to the Department Of Computing! It is with great pleasure and enthusiasm that I extend my warmest greetings to all visitors.
The aim of the Faculty of Computer Sciences is to provide a dynamic learning environment that fosters creativity, critical thinking, entrepreneurial skills, and technical excellence among students in computing disciplines. To achieve this objective, highly qualified, industry oriented and dedicated faculty members are directing students to improve their skills for the competitive positions in the progressive industry and contribute to the technology-driven economy of the country. The faculty comprises three core departments of the university i.e. Computer Science, Software Engineering, and Information Technology and offering a wide range of programs at the Diploma, Bachelor’s, Master’s, and PhD levels..

Dr. Shahid Munir Shah
